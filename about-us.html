About LagVelki

Lagvelki is a a production and distribution company. We produce and distribute films. In our site visitor also can access our film only paying for the film what they want to experience.

Lagvelki is committed to bring cult classics to award-winning masterpieces. Lagvelki is committed to flourish true form of visual arts without compromising the artist's views, thoughts & philosophical aspects. This is a platform where any artists can tell their stories without worrying about external factors. Lagvelki believes in the recognition of film makers and film producers. An industry cannot be elevated if the makers are not nurtured properly. This is why Lagvelki is promoting not only the good films but also the film makers who have potential to think something new.
